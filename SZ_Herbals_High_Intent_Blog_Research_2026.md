**What People Are Desperately Trying to Fix:**
- "My blood sugar is 280 every morning" — fasting glucose spikes

**What Medications They Want to Avoid / Are Quitting:**

**What Alternatives They Seek:**
- "Natural alternative to Ozempic without nausea"
- "Natural substitute for metformin"
- "Herbal blood sugar support that doesn't destroy my stomach"
- "Something that works without the bathroom emergencies"
- "Doctor recommended metformin but I chose something else"
- "My blood sugar went from ___ to ___" — success story seekers
- "GLP-1 alternative without injections"

**Key Emotional Triggers:**
- Fear of diabetes progression (numbness, vision loss, kidney damage)
- Humiliation from GI side effects in public
- Feeling dismissed by doctors ("just push through it")
- Frustration with "magic pill" marketing
- Desire for dignity and normal life while managing blood sugar
- Cost concerns (Ozempic $900+/month, metformin cheap but intolerable)
