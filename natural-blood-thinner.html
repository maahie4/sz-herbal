Does curcumin have side effects?

Curcumin is generally well tolerated. Mild gastrointestinal discomfort may occur at high doses. Important contraindications include: people taking prescription blood thinners (warfarin, apixaban, clopidogrel), those with bleeding disorders, gallbladder disease or bile duct obstruction, individuals scheduled for surgery within 2 weeks, pregnant or breastfeeding women, and those allergic to the Zingiberaceae (ginger) family. Always consult a healthcare provider before use.
